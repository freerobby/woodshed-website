How it works

Three steps to clarity

🎙️ 1

Record

Tap once to start. Woodshed listens in the background while you play — no fiddling with buttons between pieces.

🔬 2

Analyze

Silence detection splits your session into individual segments. Each one is fingerprinted, timed, and matched to pieces you've played before.

📈 3

Track progress

Rate each run — Learning, Rough, Steady, Solid, or Performance-ready. Watch the trend lines. Know when you're truly ready.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for serious musicians who want data, not distraction.

✂️

Auto-segmentation

Silence detection automatically breaks a long recording into individual pieces or runs. No manual tagging required.

🧬

Acoustic fingerprinting

Woodshed recognizes what you played and links it to previous performances — even if you didn't name the piece.

⭐

Self-rating system

Five levels: Learning → Rough → Steady → Solid → Performance-ready. Rate each segment and track the trajectory.

🔥

Practice streaks

Daily streaks and session history keep you accountable. The data doesn't lie — and neither does a broken streak.

🎯

Practice goals

Set daily or weekly practice targets. Woodshed tracks time and progress toward your goals automatically.

☁️

iCloud backup & widget

Your entire practice history syncs via iCloud. A home screen widget shows your streak and today's session at a glance.
