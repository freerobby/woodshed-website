Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for serious musicians who want improvement, not distractions.

🎶

Focus on the Music

Everything in Woodshed orients around your music. It enables quicker and more effective listening and analysis, without creating any distractions while you're playing.

✂️

Auto-segmentation

Woodshed automatically breaks your practice session into individual pieces and exercises, so you can easily play back and revisit the parts that need your attention. You can tune this to your personal playing habits.

🫆

Performance fingerprinting

Woodshed learns what you play and helps you link all your performances of the same piece, so you can track your progress and hear your improvement, on a piece-specific basis, with minimal effort.

⭐

Self-rating system

Five levels: Learning → Rough → Steady → Solid → Performance-ready. Set goals for yourself, rate your playing as you go, and hear your progress over time.

🙌

Personal Goals

Daily streaks, weekly targets, performance-ready pieces -- Woodshed tracks it all so you can set and measure any goal you want.

☁️

Data portability

Your practice history (including raw audio files) syncs to iCloud, so it's safe and fully yours.
